Key Features

Known for its modern skyscrapers and iconic Grande Arche.

Major Companies

Headquarters for Total, Société Générale, and other global firms.

Accessibility

Connected by the RER A and Metro Line 1.

Architectural Highlights

Home to over 60 modern skyscrapers, including Tour First.

Shopping & Dining

Features Les Quatre Temps, one of Europe's largest malls.

Green Spaces

Includes Esplanade de La Défense, a long green promenade.

Events

Hosts annual art exhibitions and business conventions.

Future Developments

Plans for more eco-friendly buildings and public spaces.
